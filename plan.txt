This appears to be a **one-page personal portfolio website** optimized for mobile, with a dark theme and orange accent color. Here's a structured plan based on the design.

# UI Design Specifications

### Colors

* Background: `#0A0A0A`
* Card Background: `#121212`
* Primary Orange: `#FF6B00`
* Accent Orange: `#FF8C00`
* Text White: `#FFFFFF`
* Secondary Text: `#B0B0B0`

### Typography

* Headings: Poppins Bold
* Body: Poppins Regular

### Effects

* Orange glow around profile image
* Card hover lift animation
* Smooth scrolling
* Animated skill bars
* Button hover glow

This layout can be implemented as a fully responsive single-page portfolio with approximately **7 sections and 10 reusable components**.
